Research Interests

Development of Executive Functions

I am interested in investigating how experience with two languages shapes domain-general abilities and how this extends into executive functions such as task-switching, inhibition control, and emotional regulation.

Word Learning

How does second language experience influences the inferences that children make when learning new words and forming new conceptual categories? How does the bilingual experience contribute to a child's ability to represent words and map them onto concepts?

Bilingual Advantage

Is there such a thing as a bilingual advantage? There have been conflicting findings among researchers in regards to this topic. I am interested in testing children across the bilingual spectrum (passive to active bilinguals) and finding the threshold at which this advantage can be seen.
