Research Interests

Executive Functions
I am interested in investigating how experience with two languages shapes domain-general abilities, such as cognitive control and emotional regulation. Word Learning
How does second language experience influences the inferences that children make when learning new words and forming new conceptual categories? Bilingual Advantage
Is there such a thing as a bilingual advantage? There have been conflicting findings among researchers in regards to this topic. I am interested in testing children across the bilingual spectrum (passive to active bilinguals) and finding the threshold at which this advantage can be seen (if there is one).
